"Hunt the Hunter"
This School pumped skilled soldiers with mutagens and elixirs to create deadly monster hybrids that can serve the empire with the same purpose and honor as any pure human. Their hybrid natures are more often than not pretty visible though.

CARD: The Aelvasso can select one of 3 mutagens as their [[School Cards]]:
Werebeast Mutagen: Fast healing, Stronger mellee attack and Hideous Appearance(con) 

Werebeast Mutagen Enchantment :

On Spawn: 
Ignore All Light Injuries.
Melee Attacks deal +2 Damage
Hideous Appearance. Some Dialogue options are locked out. Some Encounters turn aggressive 

![[Werebeast.png]]

Ethereal Mutagen: Elemental absorption (mana tokens) and damage reduction.  No Elemental Weaponry (con)

Ethereal Mutagen Enchantment:
On Spawn: 
Every time you get hit with an attack that has the elemental trait, you gain 1 mana token.
If that attack causes a injury reduce that injury 1 level (Serious => Medium => light => nothing).

You can't equip any weapon/shield or armor that have the elemental trait.
![[EtherealMutagen.png]]

Necroling Mutagen: Canibalization(mana tokens), limb regeneration and  Weakness to light and fire (con)


On Spawn: 
Every Time you kill or deal over 5 damage to an enemy, gain 1 mana token.
After an Encounter if you suffered 1 or more serious injuries, you will discard 1 mana per injury to heal it.

Attacks with the Light or Fire Trait deal +3 damage to you.
