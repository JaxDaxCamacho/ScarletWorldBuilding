Hunters are the main members of the The Scarlet Company.
Specialized mercenaries that were trained to be the ultimate monster hunters, the vanguard of humanity against the tide of horrors that live in the new continent. 

The player uses a team of (max 3) Hunters to deal with monsters and abominations.

A Scarlet Hunter is compromised of the following parameters:

Name
Hunter School
Proficiency First Discipline
Proficiency Second Discipline
HealthState
Armor

Weapon Left Hand
