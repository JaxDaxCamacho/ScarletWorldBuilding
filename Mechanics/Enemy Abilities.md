From claw to fire breath, most monsters don't need steel to spell doom to our hunters. And even then some do carry steel nonetheless.

---

## Physical Attacks

### CrushingGrasp
- **Trait:** Attack
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [4-8] damage. If target is grappled, deal +2 damage.
- **Description:** The creature grabs and crushes its prey with immense strength.

### CrushingBlow
- **Trait:** Attack
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [6-12] damage. Target must make Constitution save or be Stunned.
- **Description:** A devastating strike from a massive creature.

### DeathGrip
- **Trait:** Attack, Grapple
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [3-6] damage. Target is Grappled. While grappled, target takes [2-4] damage at start of each turn.
- **Description:** Undead strength drags victims into a crushing embrace.

### ConstrictingCoils
- **Trait:** Attack, Grapple
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [4-8] damage. Target is Grappled and Restrained. Escape requires Strength check vs creature's Evasion.
- **Description:** Serpentine coils wrap around prey, crushing the life from them.

### HarvestCut
- **Trait:** Attack
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [2-4] damage. Target suffers permanent stat reduction (-1 to chosen stat). Heals only with magical restoration.
- **Description:** A rusted sickle cuts pieces from the living, wounds that never properly heal.

### BirchFlagellation
- **Trait:** Attack
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [1-3] damage. Target gains Fear status. Cumulative.
- **Description:** Birch branches strike with supernatural force, inflicting terror with each blow.

## Venom and Corrosion

### VenomousBite
- **Trait:** Attack, Poison
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [2-4] damage. Target gains [[Poisoned]] enchantment.
- **Description:** Fangs inject toxic venom into the victim's bloodstream.

### CorrosiveVenom
- **Trait:** Attack, Acid
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [4-8] damage. Target's Armor reduced by 1 until end of encounter. Acid damage ignores half of armor.
- **Description:** Acidic venom dissolves flesh and corrodes protective gear.

### HallucinogenicVenom
- **Trait:** Attack, Poison
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [2-4] damage. Target gains [[Hallucinating]] enchantment — attacks have 50% chance to target random ally instead.
- **Description:** Venom that twists perception, making victims see enemies everywhere.

## Mental and Fear Effects

### IntimidatingDisplay
- **Trait:** Fear
- **TargetType:** All enemies in range
- **Effect:** All targets must make Willpower save or gain Fear status.
- **Description:** The creature reveals its terrifying nature, breaking the morale of its prey.

### FearAura
- **Trait:** Passive, Fear
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Enemies starting turn within range must make Willpower save or be Frightened.
- **Description:** An aura of supernatural terror surrounds the creature.

### FearManifestation
- **Trait:** Fear, Summon
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** When a child within the region is terrified, the creature gains +2 to all stats for the encounter.
- **Description:** The creature draws power from the fear of the innocent.

### FearCultivation
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** When an enemy flees, the creature gains +1 Evasion and +2 damage on next attack.
- **Description:** The creature becomes stronger when prey shows fear.

### FearFeeding
- **Trait:** Passive, Heal
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** At start of turn, heal 1 HP for each Frightened enemy.
- **Description:** Terror sustains the creature, healing its wounds.

### TerrorFeeding
- **Trait:** Passive, Heal
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** When target fails Willpower save against fear effects, heal 2 HP.
- **Description:** The creature feeds on the terror of its victims.

### SinJudgment
- **Trait:** Fear, Special
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged
- **Effect:** Target with highest cumulative injury count or guilt (story-dependent) gains Fear status and is marked for capture.
- **Description:** The creature senses wickedness and marks the guilty for punishment.

### SearingPresence
- **Trait:** Fear, Damage
- **TargetType:** All enemies in range
- **Effect:** [1-2] radiant damage. Targets must make Willpower save or be Blinded until end of turn.
- **Description:** The creature's presence burns with supernatural intensity.

### MesmerizingDance
- **Trait:** Charm, Control
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged
- **Effect:** Target must make Willpower save or move toward creature and remain motionless for 1 turn.
- **Description:** Hypnotic movements draw victims toward their doom.

### Allure
- **Trait:** Charm
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged
- **Effect:** Target must make Willpower save or become Charmed for 2 turns.
- **Description:** Supernatural beauty and allure ensnare the mind.

### AlluringMusic
- **Trait:** Charm, Control
- **TargetType:** All enemies in hearing range
- **Effect:** All targets must make Willpower save or be compelled to move toward water on their turn.
- **Description:** Irresistible music draws victims to a watery grave.

## Life Drain and Consumption

### LifeDrain
- **Trait:** Attack, Heal
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [3-5] damage. Creature heals equal to damage dealt.
- **Description:** The creature drains the life force from its victim.

### InnocenceDrain
- **Trait:** Attack, Heal
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [1-3] damage. Target gains permanent [[Jaded]] trait (cannot use Hope mechanics). Creature heals 2 HP.
- **Description:** The ability to feel wonder and joy is stolen forever.

### CalculatedCruelty
- **Trait:** Attack, Heal
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [4-6] damage. If target is already injured, deal +3 damage and heal 3 HP.
- **Description:** The creature deliberately prolongs suffering, feeding on pain.

## Transformation and Shapeshifting

### Shapeshift
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creature can assume Horse or Human form. Different forms grant different abilities.
- **Description:** The creature can take different forms to deceive prey.

### HumanDisguise
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creature appears human. Revealed when: takes damage from blessed weapons, transforms at will, or fails a disguise check.
- **Description:** The monster hides behind a mask of humanity.

### HumanForm
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creature can appear as human. All stats reduced by half in this form. Can transform as free action.
- **Description:** Even dragons can wear the shape of men.

### SizeShift
- **Trait:** Utility
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Change size from Small to Large. Gain +4 HP in Large form, -2 HP in Small form (minimum 1).
- **Description:** The creature can grow or shrink at will.

### ShadowForm
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Immune to non-magical damage. Takes double damage from radiant or blessed sources.
- **Description:** The creature is made of shadow, barely tangible.

## Water and Aquatic Abilities

### WaterDrag
- **Trait:** Grapple, Water
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee (in water)
- **Effect:** Target is submerged and begins drowning. Each turn submerged: Constitution save or gain [[Drowning]].
- **Description:** The creature drags its prey beneath the water's surface.

### DrowningEmbrace
- **Trait:** Grapple, Water
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** [2-4] damage. Target is pulled into water and begins drowning.
- **Description:** Watery arms pull victims into the depths.

### TentacleGrasp
- **Trait:** Grapple, Reach
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged (30ft)
- **Effect:** [3-6] damage. Target is Grappled at range.
- **Description:** Massive tentacles strike from the depths.

### Whirlpool
- **Trait:** Area, Water
- **TargetType:** All in 20ft radius of water
- **Effect:** All targets must make Strength save or be pulled 15ft toward creature and begin drowning.
- **Description:** Spinning creates a devastating vortex.

### KrakenBreath
- **Trait:** Warning, Area
- **TargetType:** All in 60ft cone
- **Effect:** Creates fog. All enemies have disadvantage on perception checks. Signals upcoming attack.
- **Description:** A warning that the beast is about to strike.

## Undead and Curse Abilities

### CorpseSwelling
- **Trait:** Passive, Transform
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** When below half HP, creature can swell to Large size, gaining +4 HP and +2 damage but -2 Evasion.
- **Description:** The undead corpse bloats with unnatural power.

### GuardianCurse
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** If creature is killed without completing its guardian task, it reforms in 1d4 days.
- **Description:** Death is not release for those bound by duty.

### DualHeart
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creature must be reduced to 0 HP twice to be destroyed. First "death" causes it to transform and heal to full HP.
- **Description:** Two hearts beat in one chest.

### DeathProcession
- **Trait:** Aura, Fear
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creates an aura of death. Living creatures within must hide face or be marked.
- **Description:** The procession of the dead brings cold and terror.

### CurseOfTheCross
- **Trait:** Curse
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged
- **Effect:** Target becomes the new cross-bearer. Compelled to walk procession paths each night. Constitution drain each day until death.
- **Description:** The burden of leading the damned can be passed to another.

### MarkOfDoom
- **Trait:** Curse, Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** When a marked target is seen by the procession, that target gains [[Doomed]] enchantment — will die within 1d4 days.
- **Description:** To be seen by the dead is to join them.

### GoldenTouch
- **Trait:** Attack, Curse
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** Target must make Dexterity save or turn to gold (instant death). On successful save: [4-6] damage and target gains [[Gilded]] enchantment (-2 Evasion).
- **Description:** Beauty and death intertwined — flesh becomes precious metal.

### EternalVigil
- **Trait:** Passive
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Creature cannot leave its designated area. Gains +4 Armor and +2 damage while defending that area.
- **Description:** Bound forever to guard what was entrusted.

### HollowCurse
- **Trait:** Curse
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged
- **Effect:** Target must make Willpower save or gain [[Hollow Heart]] curse — permanent -1 to all social rolls, obsession with the creature.
- **Description:** Those who mock the huldra's secret are cursed forever.

## Forest and Nature Abilities

### ForestCommand
- **Trait:** Utility
- **TargetType:** Self (affects forest)
- **Effect:** Can command local wildlife. Animals act on creature's behalf. Plants shift to assist or hinder.
- **Description:** The forest itself bends to the creature's will.

### TerritorialHowl
- **Trait:** Warning, Fear
- **TargetType:** All in hearing range
- **Effect:** All enemies gain Fear status. All wildlife in area becomes hostile.
- **Description:** A howl that warns all creatures — intruders are here.

### NatureStep
- **Trait:** Passive, Movement
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Movement through natural terrain costs half action points. Leaves no tracks.
- **Description:** The forest parts for its guardian.

### PathConfusion
- **Trait:** Utility
- **TargetType:** Area
- **Effect:** All paths within area shift. Navigation checks automatically fail. Those who attempt to leave find themselves where they started.
- **Description:** The forest becomes a labyrinth for intruders.

### TributeDemand
- **Trait:** Social
- **TargetType:** Single
- **Effect:** Demands offering. Failure results in creature becoming hostile with enhanced abilities.
- **Description:** The lord of the forest demands respect.

## Special Abilities

### NightmareRide
- **Trait:** Attack, Sleep
- **TargetType:** Single, Ranged (sleeping target only)
- **Effect:** Target suffers nightmares. Wakes with 1 injury and gains Fear status.
- **Description:** The mare rides on the chest of sleepers.

### SleepParalysis
- **Trait:** Sleep, Control
- **TargetType:** Single (sleeping target only)
- **Effect:** Target cannot wake naturally. Remains conscious but paralyzed for 1d4 hours.
- **Description:** The victim is trapped between sleep and waking.

### TheSack
- **Trait:** Grapple, Capture
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee (target must be restrained or frightened)
- **Effect:** Target is captured and removed from encounter. Rescue requires finding creature's lair.
- **Description:** The wicked are stuffed into the sack and carried away.

### ChainBind
- **Trait:** Grapple, Control
- **TargetType:** Single, Melee
- **Effect:** Target is Restrained. Escaping requires Strength check vs creature's Evasion. Chained target can be captured with TheSack.
- **Description:** Ancient chains bind the wicked.

### JoyHunter
- **Trait:** Passive, Detection
- **TargetType:** Self
- **Effect:** Can sense celebrations and gatherings of happy people within miles. Drawn to joy.
- **Description:** The creature hunts happiness itself.
