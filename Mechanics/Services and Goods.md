## Contract Payouts

### Normal Contracts
These are standard mercenary jobs available to any hired sword:

| Contract Type | Payout Range | Description |
|---------------|--------------|-------------|
| Guard duty | 50-100 Escudos | Protect location or person |
| Bounty (minor) | 50-150 Escudos | Kill or capture low-threat target |
| Escort | 75-200 Escudos | Protect traveling merchant or noble |
| Bounty (major) | 150-300 Escudos | Kill or capture dangerous target |
| Investigation | 100-250 Escudos | Find missing person or item |

### Scarlet Company Contracts
Contracts from the Fifth Holy Empire through The Scarlet Company:

| Contract Tier | Payout Range | Description |
|---------------|--------------|-------------|
| Routine | 100-300 Escudos | Standard monster clearing, patrol |
| Standard | 300-800 Escudos | Dangerous beast, multiple targets |
| Difficult | 800-2000 Escudos | Serious threat, elite monster |
| High-Risk | 2000-5000 Escudos | Exceptionally dangerous target |
| Legendary | 5000-10000 Escudos | Colony-threatening monster, ancient evil |

**Contract Modifiers:**
- Rush job: +25% payout
- Hazardous location: +50% payout
- Discretion required: +20% payout
- Empire official involvement: +30% payout
