Disciplines represent the different knowledge areas that hunters need to develop to be able to preform their duties in a efficient manner.
Being able to wield your sword or deflect a blow with a shield is part of the Martial Discipline, while being able to convoke fire from you fingers as is part of the Invocation Discipline, getting the Red God to miracle  the wounds in your chest away is part of the Ecclesiastic Discipline

When preforming any Hunter Ability, if the outcome is successful, that will increase the expertise of that hunter with that ability's Discipline.
With enough proficiency the hunter will go to the next rank of proficiency of that Discipline, unlocking new Hero Cards from that proficiency which come with new abilities.

Martial Discipline: Everything that entails hitting something until they stop moving, or preventing something to do it to you.
Cards: [[Battle Stance]], [[Charge]], [[Cleave]], [[Disengaging Kick]], [[Shield Block]], [[Pommel Strike]], [[War Shout]], [[Disarm]], [[Execute]], [[Riposte]]
![[Badge_warrior.png]]

Subterfuge Discipline: Being able to blend with the shadows and then leaving those same shadows to dispatch enemies before anyone can realize where you were.
Cards: [[Vanish]], [[Backstab]], [[Smoke Bomb]], [[Garrote]], [[Silver Spikes]]
![[Badge_assassin.png]]

Survival Discipline: Survival is about the being the fittest, in whatever environment, with whatever equipment is at hand. Being able to kill without ever being in danger.
Cards: [[Snipe]], [[Ambushing Dive]], [[Envenom Weapon]], [[Butcher]], [[Trigger Spike Trap]], [[Flask of Fire]]
![[Badge_hunter.png]]

Invocation Discipline: The volatile elemental winds are flowing through all living and dying things, being able to identify, understand and use such energies can be a powerful weapon.
Cards: [[Arching Lightning]], [[Barrier]], [[Bend Mind]], [[Flame Burst]], [[Split Image]], [[Wind Blast]], [[Absorb Elements]]
![[Badge_mage.png]]

Metamorphosis Discipline: The human condition can be altered and modified by alchemical means, to heal, to transform, to survive and to evolve into something new. Alchemy and mutagens
Cards: [[Autopsy]], [[Draught Of Flame]], [[Eye of The Cat]], [[Venomous Coating]], [[Alchemical Bomb]], [[Rapid Coagulation]], [[Unnatural Weaponry]]
![[Badge_necro.png]]

Ecclesiastic Discipline: [[The Red God]] of fire and the sun is the only god that seemed to survive the golden age the [[Fifth Holy Empire]], but his power and influence was never so powerful, with him gifting his touch to so many of the devout believers
Cards: [[Miracle Heal]], [[Incinerative Smite]], [[Blessing of Light]], [[Channel His Light]], [[Spare the Dying]], [[Divine Armor]], [[Fist of Punishment]]
![[Badge_paladin.png]]
