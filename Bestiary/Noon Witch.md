A specter that materializes only during the height of the midday sun, when shadows shrink to nothing and the heat becomes oppressive. The Noon Witch appears as an emaciated crone wrapped in tattered white linens, her face hidden beneath a wide-brimmed hat. She manifests suddenly in fields and open spaces, demanding tribute from anyone unlucky enough to encounter her.
