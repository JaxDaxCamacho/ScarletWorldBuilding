Ability Cards contain one or more Hero Abilities that your hero can use in or outside of combat.

You can activate them by clicking on them and then clicking on the target of that card.
The target can be an enemy, an ally, a location or the hero himself. Normally the Ability activated will vary depending on the target selected

The components of an Ability Cards are as follows:

Card Name
Card Description
Card Discipline
Traits
Card Action Cost
