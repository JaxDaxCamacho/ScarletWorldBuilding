Serious Injuries can spell outright for the victim. They represent serious damage to biological systems that the victim requires to function. They can also leave permanent scarring and injuries.

They can be the following:

Mangled leg (-10 Evasion and Relocation costs 2 AP)

Pierced Organ (-5 Armor, -5 Attack bonus, -5 Damage Bonus, -1 Max AP)

Brain Injury (-2 MaxAp, if the victim MaxAP is now 0 then it becomes [[Unconscious]])

Crippled Main Limb (- 10 Attack Bonus / Weapon on Main Hand is disabled)

Mutilated Off Limb ( Lose 1 of the Attack abilities / Weapon on Off hand is disabled)

Slashed Artery (10 hp per turn / get [[Exsanguinated]] effect in 2 turns)
