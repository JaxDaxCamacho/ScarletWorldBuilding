Medium Injuries can significantly impact a Hunter's combat effectiveness. Roll on the appropriate table based on the attack's trait.

## Slashing Medium Injuries (1d6)

| Roll | Injury | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Deep Gash** | 3 Blood Points per turn. Get [[Exsanguinated]] effect in 5 turns. |
| 2 | **Sliced Muscle** | -2 Damage Bonus. -1 Evasion until healed. |
| 3 | **Severed Tendon** | -3 Evasion. Cannot use reactions until healed. |
| 4 | **Flayed Arm** | -3 Attack Bonus. Weapon in affected hand has -2 damage. |
| 5 | **Open Wound** | 4 Blood Points per turn. Get [[Exsanguinated]] effect in 4 turns. |
| 6 | **Cut Ligament** | -2 Attack Bonus. -1 Max AP until healed. |

## Piercing Medium Injuries (1d6)

| Roll | Injury | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Deep Puncture** | 2 Blood Points per turn. -2 Evasion until healed. |
| 2 | **Impaled Limb** | -2 Attack Bonus. Cannot reposition until healed. |
| 3 | **Pierced Muscle** | -2 Damage Bonus. Affected limb is weakened. |
| 4 | **Stab Wound** | 3 Blood Points per turn. Deep puncture wound. |
| 5 | **Nerve Strike** | -3 Attack Bonus. Hand tremors affect aim. |
| 6 | **Through-and-Through** | 2 Blood Points per turn. -1 to all defenses. |

## Blunt Medium Injuries (1d6)

| Roll | Injury | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Fractured Rib** | -2 Armor. Painful breathing. |
| 2 | **Concussed** | -1 Max AP. [[Unconscious]] if Max AP reaches 0. |
| 3 | **Fractured Limb** | -2 Attack Bonus. Affected limb is painful to use. |
| 4 | **Crushed Joint** | -3 Evasion. Cannot reposition until healed. |
| 5 | **Cracked Bone** | -1 Evasion, -1 Attack Bonus. Compounds with further blunt hits. |
| 6 | **Deep Bruising** | +2 applied damage on subsequent hits. -1 Max AP. |
