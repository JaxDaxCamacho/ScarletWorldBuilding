Medium Injuries can actually make the victim quite more vulnerable. 

They can be the following:

Fractured leg (-3 Evasion)

Fractured Ribcage (-2 Armor)

Concussed (-1 MaxAp)

Fractured limb (-2 Attack Bonus)

Sliced Muscle (-2 Damage Bonus)

Open Wound (4 hp per turn / get [[Exsanguinated]] effect in 4 turns)
