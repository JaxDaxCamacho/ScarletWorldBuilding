Light injuries are minor wounds that can be shrugged off after combat and don't severely impact fighting ability. Roll on the appropriate table based on the attack's trait.

## Slashing Light Injuries (1d4)

| Roll | Injury | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Shallow Cut** | 1 Blood Point per turn. Heals after combat. |
| 2 | **Grazed Arm** | -1 Attack Bonus until healed. |
| 3 | **Sliced Skin** | 1 Blood Point per turn. +1 damage on subsequent slashing hits to this hunter. |
| 4 | **Superficial Slash** | No mechanical effect. Cosmetic scarring. |

## Piercing Light Injuries (1d4)

| Roll | Injury | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|

| 4 | **Needle Wound** | Minimal bleeding. No ongoing effect. |
