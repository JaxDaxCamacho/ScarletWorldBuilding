Light injuries are normally injuries that can be shrugged off after combat and don't really get in the way of fighting. On applying a light injury, we roll on the ones bellow.

they can be:

light bleeding (1 hp per turn / get [[Exsanguinated]] effect in 10 turns)

Bruised (+1 applied damage on subsequent hits)

Sprained Tendon (-1 Evasion)

Scratch (no effect)
