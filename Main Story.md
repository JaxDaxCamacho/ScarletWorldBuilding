# **ACT I — “Welcome to the Golden Lands”**

## **Story Overview**

You, a member of the elite **Scarlet Company**, have been deployed to the newly discovered continent known as **The Golden Lands**. Colonists of the **Fifth Empire** have established outposts, but attacks by strange native creatures are slowing expansion and killing settlers.

Your mission: **clear threats, learn the land, and uncover why monster activity is increasing inside the city itself.**
